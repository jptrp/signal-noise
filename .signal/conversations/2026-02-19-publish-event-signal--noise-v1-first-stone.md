---

## Signal

### 1) The brand is a *lens*, not a persona
- No “we” voice.
- Neutral observational tone.
- Declarative only when structurally justified.
- Occasional structural questions allowed.
- No persuasive/marketing cadence.

### 2) V1 restraint is a feature, not a limitation
- V1 is intentionally “not engineered.”
- No markdown pipeline, CMS, analytics, tags, feeds, dates, or extra pages.
- The site exists to house a single stone, not to optimize engagement.

### 3) “Where’s more?” is resolved via minimal affordance, not expansion
- A quiet orientation line was added to reduce ambiguity without creating new routes or content:
  - “This is the public surface. Architecture notes appear over time.”
  - “No feed. No updates. Only artifacts.”
  - “This index grows slowly. Each entry is an architectural artifact.”

### 4) Publishing discipline was upheld
- Built → placed → deployed → silent read → stop.
- No immediate iteration loop.
- No announcement pressure.

---

## Noise Avoided

- Premature “choose your own adventure” brand paths (product/consulting/platform decisions).
- Over-engineering: markdown loaders, content systems, libraries, analytics, or site complexity.
- LinkedIn-first framing or distribution-driven behavior.
- Decorative design elements (gradients, glows, iconography, stock imagery).
- Captioned/over-explained diagrams.
